My tasty chili recipe

Delicious bowl of Chili

Ingredients

  • Chili Bean
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Hamburger
  • Crushed Tomatoes
  • Garlic Seasoning, Crushed Red Pepper, Salt

    • Steps

  1. Pour Sauce, Beans, and crushed tomatoes together
  2. Brown Hamburger
  3. Add Garlic seasoning, Crushed Red Pepper, and Salt
  4. Simmer on low
